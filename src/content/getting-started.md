# Getting Started

Transform your SketchUp renders into photorealistic images in three simple steps.

## Step 1: Upload Your Render

Upload a SketchUp render or architectural visualization. We support common image formats including PNG and JPEG.

For best results:
- Use clean, well-composed renders
- Ensure good lighting in your source image
- Higher resolution inputs produce better outputs

## Step 2: Configure Your Settings

Customize how your image will be transformed:

### Lighting
Choose from preset lighting conditions:
- **Outdoor**: Sunny, Overcast, or Night
- **Indoor**: All lights on, All lights off
- **Custom**: Describe your desired lighting

### AI Model
Select your preferred AI model:
- **Gemini 3 Pro**: Highest quality, 14 credits
- **Gemini 2.5 Flash**: Fast and efficient, 4 credits

### Reference Images (Optional)
Upload up to 3 reference images to guide the style, materials, or textures.

### Edit Description (Optional)
Add specific instructions for the AI, like "add warm wooden floors" or "make the walls white brick."

## Step 3: Generate

Click Generate and watch your render transform into a photorealistic image. Results typically appear within 30-60 seconds depending on the model selected.

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start simple**: Try a basic generation before adding references or custom descriptions
2. **Be specific**: Clear, descriptive prompts yield better results
3. **Iterate**: Use your generated images as starting points for refinements
4. **Save your work**: All generations are saved to your thread history for easy access
