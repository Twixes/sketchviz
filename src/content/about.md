# About SketchViz

We're building the future of architectural visualization.

## Our Mission

SketchViz exists to democratize photorealistic rendering. We believe every architect, designer, and creator should have access to stunning visualizations without expensive software or lengthy render times.

## The Problem We Solve

Traditional rendering is slow and expensive. Architects spend hours tweaking materials, lighting, and settings to get a single photorealistic image. SketchViz transforms this process from hours to seconds.

## Built in Warsaw

SketchViz is architected in Warsaw, Poland - a city where socialist realist architecture meets cutting-edge technology. Our visual identity draws inspiration from the iconic neon signs of Marszałkowska Dzielnica Mieszkaniowa (MDM), blending retro-futuristic aesthetics with modern AI.

## Our Approach

We combine the latest advances in AI image generation with a deep understanding of architectural visualization. Every feature is designed with architects and designers in mind.

## The Team

SketchViz is built by a small, focused team passionate about design and technology. We're always looking for ways to improve - if you have feedback, we'd love to hear from you at [hello@sketchviz.app](mailto:hello@sketchviz.app).
