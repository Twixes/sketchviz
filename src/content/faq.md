# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is SketchViz?
SketchViz transforms SketchUp renders and architectural visualizations into photorealistic images using advanced AI. Upload your render, configure your settings, and get stunning results in seconds.

### What image formats are supported?
We support PNG and JPEG formats for both input and output images.

### How long does generation take?
Generation typically takes 30-60 seconds depending on the AI model selected. Gemini 2.5 Flash is faster, while Gemini 3 Pro may take slightly longer for higher quality results.

## Credits & Pricing

### How do credits work?
Credits are used each time you generate an image. Different AI models have different credit costs:
- Gemini 3 Pro: 14 credits per generation
- Gemini 2.5 Flash: 4 credits per generation

### Do credits expire?
Credits do not expire. Use them whenever you're ready.

## Privacy & Data

### Who can see my images?
Your images are private. Only you can access your uploaded renders and generated images.

### How long are images stored?
Images are stored as long as your account is active. You can delete your data at any time.

### Do you use my images to train AI?
No. We do not use your images to train AI models.

## Technical

### Why does aspect ratio matter with reference images?
Due to technical limitations with the AI models, when using reference images, you must select a fixed aspect ratio for the output.

### What makes a good reference image?
Reference images work best when they clearly show the materials, textures, or style you want to achieve. Close-up shots of materials or similar architectural styles work well.
