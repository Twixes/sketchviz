# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 29, 2025

SketchViz ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your personal information when you use our AI-powered visualization service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your Google profile picture

### 1.2 Images You Upload
- Original raw renders you upload for processing
- AI-generated photorealistic images created by our service
- Lighting parameters and optional descriptions you provide
- Associated metadata (upload timestamps, thread organization)

### 1.3 Usage Information
- Threads and generations you create
- Credits purchased and consumed
- Features you use and when you use them

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information
We automatically collect standard web server logs:
- IP address (used for security and service delivery)
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and timestamps

### 1.5 Cookies
We use essential cookies to:
- Keep you logged in (authentication)
- Maintain your session state

We do not currently use third-party analytics or advertising cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling essential cookies will prevent you from using SketchViz.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the Service:** Authenticate your account, process your images through AI, and generate visualizations
- **Manage Your Account:** Track your credits, maintain your generation history, and organize your threads
- **Generate Titles:** Automatically create descriptive titles for your image threads using AI
- **Improve the Service:** Analyze usage patterns to enhance our AI prompts and user experience
- **Communicate:** Send essential service notifications and respond to your inquiries
- **Ensure Security:** Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- **Comply with Law:** Meet legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

We do not use your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.**

We share your information only with:

### 3.1 Google Gemini AI
Your uploaded images are transmitted to Google's Gemini AI service for processing. This is essential for transforming your renders into photorealistic visualizations. Google processes these images according to their [AI Products Terms of Service](https://policies.google.com/terms/generative-ai) and [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 3.2 Infrastructure Providers
We use trusted service providers to operate SketchViz:
- **Vercel:** Hosts our service and stores your images in blob storage
- **Supabase:** Manages your account data, authentication, and database
- **Google OAuth:** Handles secure authentication

These providers access only the information necessary to perform their functions and are contractually obligated to protect your data.

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or legal process, or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights, property, or safety
- Prevent fraud or abuse
- Respond to valid government requests

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Images
**Both original and AI-generated images are retained indefinitely** to maintain your generation history and allow you to access past work at any time.

You can request deletion of:
- Specific images or threads
- Your entire account and all associated data

Contact us at hello@sketchviz.app to request deletion.

### 4.2 Account Data
- Account information is retained while your account is active
- Thread metadata and generation records are kept to provide the service
- Server logs are retained for up to 90 days for security purposes

### 4.3 Deletion Process
When you request account deletion:
- Your account information will be permanently deleted
- All your images (original and generated) will be permanently deleted
- This process is irreversible

We will complete deletion requests within 30 days.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:
- **Encrypted transmission:** All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Secure authentication:** Google OAuth 2.0
- **Access controls:** Row Level Security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data
- **Secure storage:** Protected blob storage with access tokens
- **Regular monitoring:** Security updates and monitoring for threats

However, no internet transmission or storage system is 100% secure. While we use reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 Access
You can view all your uploaded images, generated visualizations, and threads within the SketchViz interface at any time.

### 6.2 Correction
You can update your account information (name, profile picture) through your Google account settings.

### 6.3 Deletion
- **Individual content:** Delete specific images or threads through the SketchViz interface
- **Complete deletion:** Request deletion of your entire account and all data by contacting hello@sketchviz.app

### 6.4 Data Portability
You can download your images directly from SketchViz. For a complete export of your data, contact hello@sketchviz.app

### 6.5 Withdrawal of Consent
You can stop using the service and request data deletion at any time.

**We will respond to privacy requests within 30 days.**

## 7. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States, where our service providers operate. These countries may have different data protection laws.

By using SketchViz, you consent to the transfer of your information to:
- United States (Vercel, Google services)
- Other regions where our infrastructure providers operate

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for these transfers.

## 8. Children's Privacy

SketchViz is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child without proper consent, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us at hello@sketchviz.app

## 9. GDPR Rights (European Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

- **Right of Access:** Request access to your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Request limitation on how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a structured format
- **Right to Object:** Object to our processing of your data
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time

**Legal Basis for Processing:**
- **Contract Performance:** Processing necessary to provide the SketchViz service you requested
- **Legitimate Interests:** Improving our service, preventing fraud, and ensuring security
- **Consent:** When you agree to specific data uses (you can withdraw consent at any time)
- **Legal Obligations:** When required by law

To exercise your GDPR rights, contact hello@sketchviz.app
