# Careers

## We're Not Currently Hiring

We're a small, focused team and don't have any open positions at this time. However, we're always interested in connecting with talented people.

## Our Values

### Ship Fast, Iterate Often
We believe in getting things in front of users quickly and improving based on real feedback.

### Design Matters
Every pixel counts. We care deeply about user experience and visual design.

### Keep It Simple
We avoid over-engineering. The best solution is usually the simplest one that works.

### Remote-First
We work asynchronously and value clear written communication.

## Stay in Touch

If you're passionate about AI, design, or architectural visualization, we'd love to hear from you. Send us a note at [careers@sketchviz.app](mailto:careers@sketchviz.app) with a bit about yourself and what interests you about SketchViz.
